Description

Are you a Hobbit? If you are, then enjoy some delectable mushrooms in savory soy sauce with the sweetness of honey! This recipe is sure to wow you out of your pants. Share this recipe to other Hobbit friends!

Ingredients

  • 400 g Baby Bella mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 3 tbsp canola oil

Steps

  1. Cut mushrooms into thirds using a sharp knife
  2. Dice garlic
  3. Chop green onions 2 cm length
  4. Heat a pan over medium-low heat and add oil to coat
  5. Add diced garlic and stir for ten seconds
  6. Add mushrooms and increase heat to medium-high and cook for about 2-3 minutes
  7. Add soy sauce and honey and stir until mushrooms are well coated
  8. Add green onions and transfer to plate to serve
  9. Enjoy
