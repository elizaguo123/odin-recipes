Description

Call for all bell pepper lovers for this recipe! This is a super easy hands off recipe for two servings is great when you are too busy with other tasks to really watch the kitchen. It uses only two ingredients and once appliance to make a super delicious side or snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F
  2. Cut out the steps of the bell peppers and then cut the top off. Save the tops
  3. Cut the bodies of the bell peppers into thirds
  4. Dip the slices and tops into the olive oil
  5. If using a glass pan, spray pam on it. If using a metal pan, lay aluminum foil on the metal pan
  6. Lay the bell peppers onto the pan
  7. Bake for 50 minutes and make sure the bell peppers collapse before taking them out of the oven
  8. Serve and enjoy
