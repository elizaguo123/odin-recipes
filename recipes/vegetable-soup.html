Description

Need an easy but nutritious meal? Stir it up with this delicious and affordable vegetable soup recipe! This soup takes less than an hour to make and will make even the pickiest of eaters want seconds! Share this recipe for the rest of your community and spread joy to the world!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 pint of chicken stock
  • roughtly 1 cup of frozen peas
  • roughly 1 cup of frozen corn kernals
  • roughly 1 cup of frozen spinach
  • chicken (optional)
  • 2 tbsp of chili powder
  • 2 tbsp of marjoram

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and sauteed in a pot with olive oil over medium high heat until caramelized
  2. While onion is being sauteed, chop baby carrots and then add in with the onions. Cook for a bit longer
  3. Add the 1 pint of chicken stock, 2 cans of diced tomatos, and 1 can of tomatos sauce
  4. Add the corn, spinach, and peas. Stir and simmer on low heat for a few minutes
  5. Add the chili powder and marjoram
  6. Add chicken (optional step)
  7. Simmer the soup for 5 more minutes
  8. Serve and enjoy
